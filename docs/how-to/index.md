# Overview

Task-focused guides for the most common dYdX workflows.

## Included Guides

- [Fetch Market Data](fetch-market-data.md) - markets, order books, candles, trades, and height
- [Fetch Balances & Positions](fetch-balances-and-positions.md) - subaccounts, positions, and asset balances
- [Fetch Transactions](fetch-transactions.md) - fills, funding payments, and transfers
- [Place & Manage Orders](place-and-manage-orders.md) - place and cancel orders with `PrivateNode`
- [Listen To Account Updates](listen-to-account-updates.md) - stream subaccount updates from the indexer
- [Listen To Public Data](listen-to-public-data.md) - stream trades, candles, markets, and block height
